In 1973, Roe v. Wade granted a nationwide right to abortion. It helped create a path to access.

But, abortion access has rarely been a straight line — it’s full of twists, turns, and roadblocks…

...especially since the US Supreme Court gave states the power to ban abortion in 2022’s Dobbs ruling.

Since then, 14 states have completely outlawed abortion and many others dismantled access to abortion in other ways.

To illustrate how difficult it is to get abortion care, we built a maze for each state where the difficulty is calculated by the state’s abortion policies.
